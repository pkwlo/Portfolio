In the first iteration, as described in the Concept section, you were an apothecary's only student, and the gameplay loop consists of events, each taking varying amounts of time, such as studying, gathering herbs, taking villager commissions and tending to the store. In this iteration, I focused a lot on the gathering sections, thinking of areas and fields with different enemies and making a large and complex battle system. I had thought that was a little boring with no other special NPCs other than your teacher and too much depth for a game that was about relaxing, gathering herbs, and making medicines.
